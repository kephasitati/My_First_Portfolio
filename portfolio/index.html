Projects

KEDIPA Website

I had the privilege of developing and designing the Kedipa (Kenya Disability Parliamentary Association) website, a project close to my heart.
In recognition of the diverse needs of Kedipa's visually impaired members, I integrated an accessibility button to ensure equal access to the website's content and services.
This project was not just about web development; it was a commitment to digital inclusivity and advocacy for disabled individuals in Kenya.
I'm proud to have contributed to a platform that advances the cause of KEDIPA and promotes accessibility for all.


For more information, visit the KEDIPA website.

About Me

I recently worked as the IT Manager for Molars Dental Practice, a clinic with five branches across Kenya, and previously as the Technical & IT Support Manager for Trace Eastern Africa, a prominent division of the internationally acclaimed brand, Trace.
Driven by an unwavering dedication to devising cost-effective solutions in the realms of IT, broadcast, and entertainment, I aspire to leverage my substantial expertise to help businesses embrace tech to cut costs, enhance business solutions and establish a prominent standing among Africa's leading IT professionals within the next five years.
My tenure at Byce Broadcast before joining Trace was marked by significant accomplishments including spearheading successful installations and integration projects featuring cutting-edge LAWO Broadcast equipment at Royal Media Services and many other projects.
Furthermore, I am playing a pivotal role in the transformation of audio transportation technology, advocating for the adoption of budget-friendly IP-based solutions as alternatives to the more expensive satellite options widely utilized by media organizations.
With a discerning eye for innovation and a steadfast commitment to advancing Africa's technological landscape, I remain dedicated to making impactful contributions within the spheres of IT, entertainment, and broadcasting.


In addition to my technical skills, I am also an entertainer and performer with a passion for music and the arts. I have performed at various events and venues around the country and have a strong following on social media.
When I'm not working or performing, you can usually find me spending time with my beloved family and dogs, who are a constant source of joy and inspiration in my life.
I am always looking for new opportunities to learn, grow, and make a positive impact in my professional and personal endeavors.
